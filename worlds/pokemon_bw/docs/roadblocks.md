# Roadblocks

- The old man in Striaton City requires the `Parcel`
- The cut tree in Dreamyard requires `HM01 Cut` (vanilla)
- The traffic cone in Dreamyard requires the `Basement Key`
- The grunts in Pinwheel Forest require the `Loot Sack`
- The Shadow Triad member in Pinwheel Forest requires the `Dragon Skull` (kinda vanilla)
- The worker on route 4 requires the `Machine Part`
- The worker in Relic Castle (1F castleside) requires the `Explorer Kit`
- Relic Castle 5F castleside gets filled and thereby grants access to the basement after beating Ghetsis (vanilla)
- The workers in route 16 require the `Blue Card`
- Traversing Driftveil Drawbridge requires the `Tidal Bell`
- Clay will get rid of the spider webs in front of Chargestone Cave after obtaining the `Quake Badge` (kinda vanilla)
- The worker in front of Twist Mountain requires the `Jet Badge` (kinda vanilla)
- The grunts on route 8 require the `Light Stone` or `Dark Stone`, depending on the version you play (kinda vanilla)
- The officer in the gate between Opelucid City and route 11 requires `Oak's Letter`
- Each badge gate requires ***its corresponding badge***
